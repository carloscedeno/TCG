## Lessons Learned & System Compounding

1. **Transactional Stock Aborts**: In high-velocity TCG marketplaces, bulk egress must be atomic. Validating the entire lot before a single row is committed prevents "Ghost Mermas" or stock drift.
2. **Batch Item Aggregation**: Duplicate rows in a CSV must be summed before the database transaction (Rule 3) to prevent constraint violations and inventory inaccuracy.
3. **Audit Log Mandatory**: Every manual stock adjustment must have a `reason` (Eg. 'Merma', 'External Sale', 'Damage') to maintain LEYE 1 (Integrity).
